Expansion Joints

Expansion joints accommodate thermal expansion and contraction in structures like buildings, bridges, and roads. They prevent damage from temperature changes and can isolate sections from vibrations caused by heavy machinery.

Elastomeric Bearings

Used in bridge construction, these bearings provide flexible support that accommodates movement while reducing stress on substructures. They're also used in seismic isolation systems to protect structures from earthquakes.
